How to verify:

  1. Download the Monthly Summary Report from Amazon Seller Central
  2. Find the "Net Proceeds" or "Total" row
  3. Compare with Net Income shown here (before COGS & OpEx)
  4. Amazon's figure = Sales − Fees − Refunds + Reimbursements
  5. Difference should be < ₹100 (rounding)

Metric Definitions & Formulas ▼

Net Margin

Net Income / Gross Sales

Percentage of revenue remaining after all costs and taxes.

ROI (Return on Investment)

Net Income / Total COGS

Return generated on inventory capital deployed.

ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)

Ad Spend / Ad-Attributed Sales

Efficiency of ad-spend relative to the sales it generated.

TACOS (Total ACOS)

Total Ad Spend / Gross Sales

Impact of advertising on total business revenue.

Total Blended Profit

Net Income - Allocated Operating Expenses

Profit after factoring in overheads like salaries, rent, and software.
